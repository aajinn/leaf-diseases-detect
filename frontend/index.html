Why Early Detection Matters

Detecting diseases early can save crops, reduce losses, and protect food security

Early Detection Benefits

  • Up to 90% reduction in crop losses
  • Lower treatment costs (50-70% savings)
  • Prevents disease spread to other plants
  • Reduces pesticide use by 60%

Late Detection Consequences

  • Complete crop failure possible
  • Disease spreads to entire field
  • Higher treatment costs and effort
  • Contamination of soil and equipment
